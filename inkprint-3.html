What gets flagged and why

Generic opener

Not wrong — just what everyone says. One specific reason beats "I am writing to express my interest" every time.

Empty claim

Nobody would argue the opposite — "communication is key" — so it says nothing. Replace with a specific example.

Unverified authority

"Research shows" with no source. Cite it or rewrite it as your own observed belief.

Vague intensifier

"Significantly improved" without a number. Every vague word can become a concrete before/after.

Filler transition

"Furthermore", "With that in mind" — delete them. The next sentence reads better without them.

Hollow closing

"I look forward to contributing" — every LLM ends this way. Say something specific about next steps instead.

Agent missing

Passive voice erases you from your own story. "Was delivered" by whom? Put yourself back in the sentence.

Circular definition

"My leadership approach is about leading effectively" — begins and ends at the same place. Say what you actually do.
