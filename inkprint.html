What gets flagged and why

Empty claim

Sentences nobody would argue against — "communication is key" — say nothing. The test: could anyone argue the opposite? If not, it's empty.

Unverified authority

"Research shows" and "experts agree" without a source. Either cite the actual study — or rewrite it as your own observed belief.

Vague intensifier

"Significantly improved" is a placeholder for a specific fact you haven't found yet. Every vague word can become a number or concrete before/after.

Filler transition

"Furthermore", "With that in mind" — words that exist to sound like writing is progressing. Delete them. The next sentence almost always reads better without them.
